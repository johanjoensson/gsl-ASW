Detailed Description

Class used to describe the structure of a crystal.
Contains:
Rn_vecs - Lattice vectors of the crystal (up to a maximum lentgh).
Kn_vecs - Reciprocal lattice vectors of the crystal (up to a maximum length).
lat - The lattice of the crystal.
atoms - The atoms in the unit cell of the crystal (the base of the crystal).
